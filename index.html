About Me

I'm a passionate web developer and entrepreneur based in Johannesburg, South Africa, dedicated to crafting clean, accessible, and visually compelling digital experiences. My journey started with curiosity — pulling apart websites to understand how they ticked — and grew into a full-blown love for the craft. I blend design sensibility with engineering rigor, building products that are as thoughtful under the hood as they are on the surface. When I'm not pushing pixels or writing code, I'm exploring startup ideas, reading about systems thinking, and drinking too much coffee. I believe great work lives at the intersection of empathy, creativity, and relentless iteration.
